About Us

We are Tanner and Naveed, two high school friends from Chicago who started building together long before AI was a buzzword. Ten years ago, we were coding manual HTML websites the old-school way, line by line, learning through trial and error, fueled by curiosity and way too much coffee. Back then, delivering a site took weeks. We were obsessed with figuring things out, breaking things, rebuilding them better, and pushing our limits every single project. That mindset never left.

When AI began changing the landscape, we did not hesitate. We leaned in completely. What used to take a month now takes an hour. What once required layers of manual effort can now be engineered into intelligent systems. We believe this shift is real, and it is massive. We work hard because we genuinely love technology, automation, and solving complex problems. Today, we build AI infrastructure for companies across the UAE, USA, India, Serbia, Pakistan, the UK, Malaysia, and beyond. We are still those same two friends who love building, just operating at a completely different level.
